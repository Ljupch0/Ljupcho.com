AEX Stock Index Dashboard

Dashboard which presents daily data of the AEX Amsterdam stock index. It's built with shinydashboard and pulls data by scraping from finance.yahoo.com, wikipedia.org, and other websites. The dashboard features several interactive visualizations such as d3treeR, Datatable, and a dygraph presenting historical data.

Latest daily price changes of the Macedonian MBI10 Stock Index

This visualization scrapes data from the latest day of stock market trading. In the treemap visualization, the size of the squares represents the market capitalization of the firm, and the color is generated by the price change of the day.
