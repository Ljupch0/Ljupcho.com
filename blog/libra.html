The Libra Opportunity

Libra will be more of a globalized payment method than a real cryptocurrency. Entrepreneurs can step in to handle the on-ramp and charge conversion fees.

Communication Networks vs Payment Networks

Libra is facebook's fulfillment of its long standing envy towards WeChat, China's utility app that has provided a popular digital payments service since XXX. Networks effects around communication and chat are one pillar which makes an app hihgly defensible, however they are complemented by the network effects that come from the number of users that pay in and the number of merchants that accept a certain payment method. Payment NFX are complementary to communication NFX because in addition to P2P money transfers between users, transactions can take place between users and merchants. Facebook already has the users for P2P, and having the users will attract the merchants. Simply having all your friends in one app makes you stay, but also getting paid there cements you as a user.


Paypal understood well network effects in payment systems and invented the growth hack that every funded fintech startup (and airbnb) is using today: invite a friend, both of you get 10 dollars. The success of this hack in getting users, coupled with the success with getting merchants via Ebay, has cemented Paypal as the dominant online payment method to this day.
